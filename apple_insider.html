MacBook Pro 14-inch (2023) Prices

Disclosure: If you buy through our links, we may earn commission as an affiliate & Amazon Associate.

The 2023 MacBook Pro 14-inch finally receives its upgraded M2 Pro and M2 Max chips, boasting performance benchmarks that blow away last year's model. The new chips can support up to 96GB of unified memory and 8TB of SSD storage for a performance boost of up to 15.9x faster during ProRes transcoding.

While the design didn't change from the M1 MacBook Pro 14-inch, the HDMI port now supports multi-channel audio output, and the M2 chips promise support for up to four external displays. Altogether, with 18 hours of battery life, the M2 MacBook Pro 14-inch is a great workhorse machine for videographers, photographers, or anyone looking for robust performance.
